The goal of this website is to make the activities of Edmonton City Council more accessible!

You can search for terms you are interested in the Search tab as well as view the meetings by year by clicking on one of the years on the sidebar.

To get notified when new meeting information is released you can add our RSS feed to any RSS reader.

To only be notified on certain subjects we also have RSS feeds for:

  • Health
  • Taxes
  • Safety

This website parses City Council meeting minutes from https://pub-edmonton.escribemeetings.com.

This project is open source. You can view the code at https://github.com/SamuelmdLow/EdmontonCityCouncilFeed
